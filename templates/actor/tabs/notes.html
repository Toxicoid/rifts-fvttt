Quick Reference

Melee Combat

  • Strike — Roll d20 + strike bonus vs defender's parry/dodge
  • Parry — Roll d20 + parry bonus to block incoming attack
  • Dodge — Roll d20 + dodge bonus; costs one attack
  • Roll w/ Impact — Roll d20 + bonus to halve damage from falls/blasts
  • Pull Punch — Roll d20 + bonus to reduce damage dealt
  • Critical Strike — Natural {{system.combat.criticalOn}}+ = double damage
    • {{#if system.combat.knockoutOn}}
  • Knockout — Natural {{system.combat.knockoutOn}}+ = KO
    • {{/if}}

Saving Throws

  • vs. Magic — Roll d20, need 12+ (bonus: {{system.saves.magicBonus}})
  • vs. Psionics — Roll d20, need 12+ (bonus: {{system.saves.psionicBonus}})
  • vs. Poison — Roll d20, need 14+ (bonus: {{system.saves.poisonBonus}})
  • vs. Horror Factor — Roll d20, need HF score (bonus: {{system.saves.horrorBonus}})
  • vs. Insanity — Roll d20, need 12+ (bonus: {{system.saves.insanityBonus}})
